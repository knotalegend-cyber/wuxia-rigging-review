How to review
  1. Tap image → fullscreen zoom.
  2. Change category via dropdown if classifier got it wrong. Useful categories: head, hair, torso, arm_upper, arm_lower, arm_full (Scenario sometimes gives one piece for the whole shoulder-to-wrist arm), hand, leg_upper, leg_lower, leg_full, foot, eye, mouth, brow.
  3. Tap ✓ OK when the sprite + category are correct and this is the canonical pick for its slot.
  4. Tap Variant for duplicates (e.g. one of 4 hand-palms). Use the comment field to note which cell it's a duplicate of, or any other detail.
  5. Tap Reject for noise / stray fragments / cells not usable at all.
  6. Comment field is always available — use it for anything you'd tell me verbally ("rotate 5° CCW before wiring", "double-check thumb on this one", "crop 4px off the bottom").
  7. When done, tap Export JSON (full structured data) or Copy Summary (readable text). Then tap Copy to Chat.
  8. Decisions + comments persist on-device — closing and reopening this page keeps your state.
